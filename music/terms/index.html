Usage terms of my music

I believe that my music should benefit the world, however I'd want to set a few rules.

My music is licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0.

DOs and DON'Ts

Do these cool things

  • Use my music privately or even commercially in a game, video etc. and state who's the author (me)

And don't do these

  • Use, remix and adapt my music without attribution
